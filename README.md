# Freelance Data & AI Market Intelligence Platform

A portfolio-quality market intelligence platform that collects, processes,
models and analyzes freelance and remote-work job postings to support
data-driven decisions about learning, freelancing, consulting and future
business opportunities.

## Project Objectives

The platform will help answer:

- Which data and AI services are most demanded?
- Which skills and tools are most valuable?
- Which industries are hiring?
- Which niches offer strong financial opportunities?
- Which opportunities are suitable for beginners?
- Which skills should be prioritized?
- Which freelance and consulting services should be developed?
- Which future products or businesses could emerge?

## Planned Architecture

1. Ethical data collection
2. Raw data landing
3. Data validation and quality control
4. Cleaning and standardization
5. PostgreSQL warehouse
6. Analytics engineering and KPI modeling
7. Business intelligence dashboards
8. Data science and NLP
9. AI-assisted market intelligence
10. Learning, freelance, consulting and business recommendations

## Technology Strategy

This project follows an OSS-first approach:

- Use established open-source tools where they solve the requirement well.
- Configure and integrate tools before building replacements.
- Write custom code only for project-specific gaps.
- Keep the architecture reproducible and portfolio-ready.

## Current Status

Environment and repository foundation are being established.

## Data Governance

Raw source data, credentials and restricted job descriptions will not be
published in this repository. Public outputs will consist of code,
documentation, schemas, aggregated results and sanitized samples.
